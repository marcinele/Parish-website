Holy Family Parish in Borki

About us

Holy Family Parish is a vibrant community. This vibrancy comes from our exciting and meaningful experiences and relationships in the parish. Our parish family is unique and is a reflection of the varied cultures and backgrounds of our community. We are not a parish that is active on weekends only, but through numerous Ministries, groups and programs; St. Michael’s is alive and thriving every day and evening of the week.

History of our parish

The religious life of the village was organized in a small chapel. The construction of the new church was started in 1981 according to the design of Zbigniew Zjawin. Bishop Jerzy Ablewicz consecrated and laid the foundation stone on October 18, 1981. The church was built as a votive offering for saving the life of the Holy Father. John Paul II, therefore the Father of St. in 1982 he donated a statue of Our Lady of Fatima consecrated by him. The consecration of the church took place on October 13, 2006 under the leadership of Bishop Wiktor Skworc. On May 13, 1992, the church was elevated to the rank of a sanctuary by the decree of Bishop Józef Życiński. The interior was designed and made by Józef Furdyna.
